Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

SubSentry ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your email address and profile information via Google OAuth to authenticate your account and sync data across devices.
  • Subscription Data: We collect information about your digital subscriptions (service name, price, renewal dates) that you manually enter or that the extension detects to provide tracking and AI insights.

2. Use of Permissions

To provide our services, SubSentry requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: Used to maintain a local cache of your subscription data for a fast and responsive user interface.
  • Alarms & Notifications: Used to schedule and display alerts 24 hours before your free trials or subscriptions expire.
  • Identity: Used to securely sign you in and connect your data to your Firebase account.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase/Firestore. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Payments: All payment transactions are processed securely by Paddle. We do not store or have access to your credit card or financial information.
  • Authentication: We use Google OAuth for secure login; your Google password is never shared with us.

5. Data Deletion

Users have the right to delete their account and all associated data at any time through the extension settings or by contacting us directly.
